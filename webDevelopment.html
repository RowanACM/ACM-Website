Web Development Team


The ACM Web Development Team is a hard working and growing team in the ACM. Our goal is to teach the fundumentals of Web Development
and that the Web Development is more than just building websites. In today's world almost everythng you use is some form of web application.
Understanding Web Development is extremely important because it is the foundation of todays tech industry. While our team does build web sites
on the side as favors to other clubs, we also strive to build powerful web applications that interact with backends and databases in order to help
better prepare ourselves for the real world and better educate oursleves in an area of the field of computer science.
